Foaming Fury

Foaming Fury is a thrilling white water rafting trip that takes you on a wild ride through the rapids of a scenic river. You will experience the adrenaline rush of paddling through grade 3 rapids, surrounded by stunning scenery and wildlife. Foaming Fury is suitable for beginners and experienced rafters alike, as you will be guided by professional and friendly instructors who will ensure your safety and enjoyment. Foaming Fury is a full-day / half-day adventure that includes transfers, equipment, snacks and drinks. Foaming Fury is the ultimate way to experience the beauty and excitement of white water rafting.

Torrential Thrills

Torrential Thrills is a white water rafting trip that will challenge you to the limit. You will face the most intense rapids of a raging river, with twists and turns that will test your skills and courage. Torrential Thrills is for experienced rafters only, as you will need to have a high level of fitness and stamina. You will be accompanied by expert instructors who will guide you through the extreme adventure. Torrential Thrills is a full-day trip that includes transfers, equipment, lunch and drinks. Torrential Thrills is the ultimate challenge for white water rafting enthusiasts.

Raging Rapids

Raging Rapids is a white water rafting trip that will give you a fun and exhilarating experience. You will enjoy the thrill of paddling through moderate rapids of a lively river, with splashes and drops that will make you laugh and scream. Raging Rapids is for anyone who loves adventure, as you will have a great time with your friends and family. You will be assisted by friendly instructors who will make sure you have a safe and enjoyable trip. Raging Rapids is a half-day trip that includes transfers, equipment, snacks and drinks. Raging Rapids is the perfect way to have fun and relax on the river.
