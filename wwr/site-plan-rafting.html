Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to increase visibility and reach, share with our customers information about the values of our company and the services and activities we provide, and facilitate bookings and reservations.

Audience

Our target audience is anyone older than 12 years old.

What do they want?
Our customers are interested in developing and strengthening bonds with their families or friends by having fun and going on aquatic adventures whilst ensuring their safety.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
Our target audience expects to find information about the trips we provide, what to expect from them, our history, and contact information for bookings and further questions.

What are their needs that aren't being met?
Without this website, our customers will not have an easy way to visualize the trips provided, reviews by others on them, and information about us without contacting us directly.
