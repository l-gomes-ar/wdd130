Rapids Rafting

Rapids Rafting is a company that offers exciting and memorable white water rafting trips in Wales. Our motto is 'Ride the Rapids, Feel the Fun'. Our core values are: adventure, safety, quality, and satisfaction.

History

Rapids Rafting was founded in 1998 by John Gomez and Jess Smith, a couple who shared a passion for rafting and nature. They started with one raft and a few friends, and soon they attracted more customers who wanted to experience the thrill of the rapids. They expanded their fleet and hired more instructors, who were trained and certified by the British Canoe Union. They also partnered with local hotels and restaurants to offer packages and discounts to their clients.

Rapids Rafting has grown to become one of the most popular and reputable rafting companies in Wales. We have a variety of trips to suit different levels of skill and interest, from gentle to extreme. We operate on several rivers in Wales. We have a loyal customer base who come back year after year to enjoy our trips. We also welcome new customers who want to try something different and adventurous. Rapids Rafting is more than just a company, it is a family of rafting enthusiasts who love to share their passion with others.
